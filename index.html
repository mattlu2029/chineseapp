How to Practice

1. Watch the left square — it shows the finished character and its stroke order (click "Replay stroke order" to watch again)


2. Write in the right square — a faint copy of the character sits underneath as a guide


3. Your ink stays — your real strokes aren't corrected, so you can compare them against the faint correct shape


4. Clear and repeat — "Clear" wipes your ink; "Got it" marks the character learned

Stroke Order Rules
• Top before bottom
• Left before right
• Horizontal before vertical (when crossing)
• Outside before inside
• Center before sides (in symmetric chars)
